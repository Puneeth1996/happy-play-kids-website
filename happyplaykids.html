About Us

We Happy Play Kids located at Vidyaranyapura, Bangalore, Karnataka, are one of the best schools providing services like play group, day care centre, teacher training programme and many more. We even organise fun and interesting summer camps for our students. In our summer camp we make sure that children are safe, comfortable and have all the necessary things to make them feel like home. In our school we provide students the utmost quality education through our well crafted curriculum. We focus on the overall development of every child through extra-extracurricular activities. We make sure your kid gains the competitive edge.
